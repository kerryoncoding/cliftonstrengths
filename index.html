CliftonStrengths for Managers

Assessment taken on 05-06-2019 by Kerry Guarino

  1. Consistency: Create fair systems to establish and build trust.
  2. Maximizer: Strive for excellence, and encourage others to do the same.
  3. Relator: Connect deeply with the right people to gain friends for life.
  4. Achiever: Bring intensity and effort to the most important areas of your life.
  5. Arranger: Improve effectiveness and efficiency by reorganizing resources.
  6. Responsibility: Take ownership for things that matter most to you.
  7. Learner: Use your passion for learning to add value to your own and others' lives.
  8. Discipline: Create structure, and keep things organized.
  9. Empathy: Appreciate and refine your gift for understanding others' thoughts and feelings.
  10. Intellection: Think deeply. Think often.
